EN Edge Runtime Secure Gateway

FeedOS Evidence Node

A hardened edge runtime to acquire telemetry from sensors/PLC/SCADA with compliant buffering. Data is pre-validated (timestamps, integrity, bounds) and signed at source before being sent to the cloud — preserving the chain of evidence.

  • Industrial & IoT protocols (Modbus, MQTT, REST)
  • Health checks and local buffering for network outages
  • Digital signing of data packets at the origin

AI Digital Twin Optimization

Digital Twin & Evidence Compiler

Outcome prediction and optimization engines combine feed formulation, environmental telemetry, and lab assays to model nutrition outcomes (e.g., Omega-3) and sustainability (CO₂e). The output is a standardized pre-dossier ready for claim issuance.

  • Linear/Stochastic optimization with cost/circularity/welfare constraints
  • Closed-loop learning with assay feedback
  • Traceable feature lineage for audits

QR FCS™ Dossier Signed Claims

Outcome Dossier & QR

For each batch, FeedOS produces a digitally-signed Outcome Dossier (FCS™) that includes the claim payload, telemetry lineage, and cryptographic signature. A retailer or auditor can validate instantly via QR or open JSON.

  • Machine-verifiable JSON + human-friendly view
  • Tamper simulation for training and QA
  • Deep-link from the operations panel

API Retail/Reg EFSA-aligned

Verification API

A lightweight API to validate claims in retail systems, marketplaces, and customs/regulatory workflows. Send a claim_token and receive valid / invalid / tampered.

  • Rate-limited and auditable
  • Webhooks for eventing (e.g., shelf label updates)
  • SDKs (JS / Python) coming soon
